Classification Game

You have 20 cards providing information about houses in your training set.

  • 10 give information about properties in San Francisco
  • 10 give information about properties in New York

You can download the training set as a csv containing the data that you can open in Excel if you feel it would help.

Your challenge is to use this data to inform the building of a decision tree that can classify cards where the city is unknown (the evaluation set).

You can rearrange the all sets of cards within the panel by dragging them.

Please design your decision tree in the panel available.

When you have finished building your tree, you can select your own evaluation set to see if it works.

Buttons

Run evaluation: Try your tree with the cards.

Reset: Moves the cards back into the panel after an evaluation.

Good luck!
